Women's Safety Guide & Awareness

Legal Rights

  • Right to file FIR for any cognizable offense (Section 154 CrPC)
  • Protection from domestic violence under DV Act 2005
  • Sexual harassment protection under POSH Act 2013
  • Right to free legal aid if income is below prescribed limit
  • Zero FIR can be filed at any police station regardless of jurisdiction

Personal Safety Tips

  • Always inform someone about your whereabouts
  • Keep emergency numbers on speed dial
  • Trust your instincts
  • Avoid sharing personal information with strangers
  • Use well-lit, populated routes
  • Keep your phone charged

Self-Defense Basics

  • Target vulnerable areas: eyes, nose, throat, groin
  • Use your voice loudly
  • Carry pepper spray (where legal)
  • Learn basic martial arts
  • Create distance and run to safety

Cyber Safety

  • Use strong unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Be cautious sharing personal photos
  • Report cyber harassment
  • Block suspicious accounts
  • Never share OTP or banking details

Workplace Safety

  • Know your company's POSH policy
  • Document incidents with dates and witnesses
  • File written complaints
  • No retaliation allowed by law
  • Contact NCW if internal process fails
